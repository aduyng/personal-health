Premium Campaign Redemptions

The below section is dedicated to the exported data of the Premium Campaign Redemptions.
Campaign redemption contains information about campaigns used to purchase subscriptions.

Files Included:
----------
UserPremiumCampaignRedemptions.csv

The data for premium campaign redemptions

    subscription_id                                - id of the subscription
    campaign_redemption_id                         - id of the campaign redemption
    create_time                                    - create time of the campaign redemption, timestamp
    campaign                                       - campaign name
    campaign_redemption_type                       - campaign redemption type, e.g. 'CAMPAIGN_REDEMPTION_TYPE_PURCHASE'
