In-app Notification Messages Data Export

In-app Notification Messages Export provides a way to export your in-app notification messages.
The data is exported in CSV format.

Files Included:
----------

inapp-notification-messages.csv - The CSV file containing the In-app Notification Messages.

This file contains the following columns:
  id                              - The unique identifier of the In-app Notification Message.
  title                           - The title of the In-app Notification Message.
  body                            - The body of the In-app Notification Message.
  priority                        - The priority of the In-app Notification Message.
  destination                     - The destination id of the In-app Notification Message.
  creation_time                   - The creation time(Unix timestamp) of the In-app Notification Message.
  expiration_time                 - The expiration time(Unix timestamp) of the In-app Notification Message.
  left_action_text                - The left action text of the In-app Notification Message.
  right_action_text               - The right action of the In-app Notification Message.
  entity_id                       - The entity id of the In-app Notification Message.
  entity_type                     - The entity type of the In-app Notification Message.
