Time Series Data Export

The Time Series export provides a detailed timeline of your tracked activity.

Files Included:
----------

vertical_ratio_YYYY-MM-DD.csv         - Where YYYY-MM-DD is the starting date for the entries in the file.

Vertical ratio measures your running efficiency. It's calculated as vertical oscillation divided by stride length.
Vertical oscillation is the amount of vertical movement of your body during a stride.
Each entry has the following values:

    timestamp                         - Date and time at which the entry was logged.
    vertical ratio                    - Vertical ratio value (in percentage).
