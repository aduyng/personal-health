Time Series Data Export

The Time Series export provides a detailed timeline of your tracked activity.

Files Included:
----------

ground_contact_time_YYYY-MM-DD.csv     - Where YYYY-MM-DD is the starting date for the entries in the file.

Each entry has the following values:

    timestamp                         - Date and time at which the entry was logged.
    ground contact time               - The amount of time for which the foot was in contact with the ground on that stride, in milliseconds.
