Time Series Data Export

The Time Series export provides a detailed timeline of your tracked activity.

Files Included:
----------

vertical_oscillation_YYYY-MM-DD.csv   - Where YYYY-MM-DD is the starting date for the entries in the file.

Vertical Oscillation is the distance off the ground your center of mass moves with each stride while running.
Each entry has the following values:

    timestamp                         - Date and time at which the entry was logged.
    vertical oscillation              - Vertical oscillation in centimeters.
